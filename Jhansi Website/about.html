    Our Honourable Founder

    Jhansi High School aims of imparting quality education to the children along with moral values. In the 32 years of the establishment,the school has emerged as a pioneer in the field of education under the dynamic leadership and guidance of our founder-Head mistress M.Jhansi Rani,who herself is a renowned academician.

    Jhansi High School was started in the year 1980 when there were no English medium Schools in this locality,ever since the school rept improving and gave quality education to thousands of students.Every year improvements are done according to the needs of the students and make them best citizens of the society and the nation.

    IIT-JEE foundation for classes 6th to 10th is introduced.Computer classes.Yoga,Karate,Dancing&Singing classes are conducted.To make them best citizensdiscipline is maintained. Instead of valentine day we are celebrating Matru Pitru Pooja on Feb 14th.

    Cash award is given to the students who scored highest marks in S.S.C exams.Qualified teachers are appointed.Educational tours are organised class wise every year. Physical check-up also under taken by our madam.Doctors from Lion's club are coming regularly and doing eye,dental check-up and Spectacles are also distributed if nesassary.Every year we are conducting seminars,giving project works,conducting cultural activities like cooking,vegetable carviing,mehandi competition etc.essay writing & Drawing competitions are conducted regularly.

    The management takes care for the over all development of the students.
